Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Semyon, a passionate web developer and competitive swimmer hailing from Ireland.

When I'm not coding up sleek, responsive websites, you can find me perfecting my freestyle technique, always chasing that sub-one-minute mark in the 100-meter race.

A lover of all things science fiction, I often find myself dreaming about futuristic worlds, where my HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills might just come in handy for building a spaceship interface—or at least a cool sci-fi-themed website.

In between swims and coding sessions, I like to challenge myself with a good game of chess, proving that strategy is just as important on the board as it is in life (and web development!).

And when I need a break from the screen, you'll find me in the workshop, turning pieces of wood into something functional and beautiful—because who says a coder can't also be a craftsman?

As I dive into my college journey, I'm excited to expand my knowledge, debug all the bugs, and build some web apps that are worth showing off.

I'm actively involved in the university community as a member of CompSoc (Computer Society), where I've served as Public Relations Officer, Ordinary Committee Member, and Auditor. It's been rewarding to help organize events, manage society operations, and connect with fellow students who share my passion for technology.

Whether it's in the pool, on the web, or in the workshop, I believe in pushing limits and thinking creatively.

Let's build something awesome together!
